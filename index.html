Learn more

BreakPet is a simple program that reminds you to take a small break.

Following the 20-20-20 rule, BreakPet notifies you every 20 minutes to take a 20 second long eye break.

Small and unintrusive

BreakPet simply lives on the dock, popping in when it's time for a visual break.

Simple and easy to use

All you have to do is launch BreakPet, click start, and that's it! No further setup is required.

Made in Swift

BreakPet was made for Mac. Written in Swift, BreakPet has a small footprint by design.

Adaptive Theming

BreakPet automatically themes itself to your system settings, so it is cohesive with your setup.
